# 2026 Seasonal Content Map

Use this as the high-level monthly planning map for Pinterest, affiliate content, seasonal product research, and related campaign timing.

## January

* New Year reset
* organization
* planners
* self-improvement
* winter self-care
* cozy home
* Valentine’s prep starts late month
* Jewish:
  * no major high-volume retail overlap

## February

* Valentine’s Day
* Galentine’s
* self-care
* beauty
* gifts under `$25-$50`
* cozy/home/date-night vibes
* Jewish:
  * `Purim` prep can begin late depending on timing, but not a major broad retail month unless you choose to niche in

## March

* Easter prep starts
* spring refresh
* organization
* outdoor prep
* early travel prep
* kids basket fillers
* Jewish:
  * `Purim` can matter
  * `Passover` prep starts depending on year and timing

## April

* spring
* outdoor
* beauty
* travel prep
* Easter
* garden/backyard
* Jewish:
  * `Passover`
  * strongest Jewish overlap in spring for home/hosting/family content

## May

* Mother’s Day
* graduation
* teacher gifts
* early summer
* beauty / gifting / hosting
* Jewish:
  * `Shavuot` if you want niche hosting/family/table content

## June

* weddings
* bridal/bachelorette
* travel
* summer beauty
* hosting
* outdoor/home upgrades
* Father’s Day
* Jewish:
  * not a major broad commerce month

## July

* Fourth of July
* pool
* backyard
* road trips
* travel essentials
* summer must-haves
* Jewish:
  * not a major broad commerce month

## August

* back-to-school
* dorm/apartment setup
* teacher prep
* organization reset
* late-summer travel
* Jewish:
  * light / niche only

## September

* fall decor
* cozy home
* Halloween ramp
* organization reset
* beauty transition to fall
* Jewish:
  * `Rosh Hashanah`
  * `Yom Kippur`
  * `Sukkot`

## October

* Halloween
* costumes
* spooky/cute
* fall hosting
* cozy home
* Jewish:
  * early `Simchat Torah` overlap from the late-September holiday cycle

## November

* Thanksgiving
* hosting
* kitchen/dining
* Black Friday
* Cyber Monday
* gift guides begin hard
* Jewish:
  * usually not a main retail overlap month for this content system

## December

* Christmas
* Hanukkah
* stocking stuffers
* gift guides
* cozy winter
* New Year’s prep
* Jewish:
  * `Hanukkah`

## Highest Jewish-Holiday Crossover Months

For this system, the most useful Jewish-holiday planning months are:

* April
* September
* December
