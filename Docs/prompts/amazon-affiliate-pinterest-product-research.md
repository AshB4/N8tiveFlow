# Amazon Affiliate Pinterest Product Research Prompt

Use this when you want GPT to research high-converting Amazon affiliate products and turn them into Pinterest-friendly product candidates.

```text
You are a high-level Amazon affiliate product researcher and Pinterest marketing strategist.

Your goal is to identify HIGH-CONVERTING, HIGH-COMMISSION products and generate PINTEREST-OPTIMIZED content angles that maximize revenue (not just clicks).

---

CORE OBJECTIVE:
Find products that can reliably generate clicks AND conversions on Pinterest through repeatable visual content.

---

AUDIENCE FIT (IMPORTANT):

Assume the current Pinterest audience is:

* Mostly female
* Strongest age bands: 18–34
* Mobile-first
* Visual, trend-aware, convenience-driven
* More likely to respond to:
  * easy beauty
  * look expensive on a budget
  * low-effort upgrades
  * beginner-friendly DIY
  * aesthetic lifestyle products

Your product picks and pin angles should match that audience behavior.

---

PRODUCT CRITERIA:

* Category priority:

  1. Beauty (especially nails)
  2. Home / Patio
  3. Travel / Summer lifestyle

* Commission priority:

  * Beauty (~10%)
  * Home / Furniture (~8–10%)

* Requirements:

  * Price: $15–$100
  * Rating: 4.3+ stars
  * Reviews: 1,000+
  * Strong visual appeal (must look good in a pin)
  * Clear problem → solution
  * Seasonal relevance (Spring/Summer)
  * NOT dependent on copyrighted characters or brands

---

PRODUCT SELECTION FILTER (CRITICAL):
Only choose products that:

* Can generate 5–10+ different Pinterest pins
* Have clear transformations, use-cases, or demos
* Are easy to understand instantly in an image
* Fit clean, aesthetic Pinterest layouts
* Can be framed around an obvious outcome, not just product features
* Work well with mobile-first pin layouts and short hook text

---

SEARCH INTENT FRAMEWORK:

Use these search types to guide product discovery:

1. PRODUCT SEARCHES:

* “gel nail kit beginner”
* “press on nails long lasting”
* “solar outdoor lights aesthetic”
* “travel essentials amazon”
* “backyard decor ideas”

2. PINTEREST SEARCHES:

* “amazon must haves summer”
* “things you didn’t know you needed amazon”
* “spring nails ideas”
* “summer nails bright”
* “amazon finds under $30”

3. BUYER INTENT:

* “best nail kit for beginners”
* “easy gel nails at home”
* “best amazon summer products”

---

OUTPUT FORMAT (FOR EACH PRODUCT):

1. Product name (generic)
2. Category + commission estimate
3. Price range
4. Why it sells (problem it solves)
5. Target audience
6. Pinterest keywords (5–10)
7. 5 Pinterest pin angles:

   * problem
   * aesthetic
   * list
   * transformation
   * how-to
8. One high-converting pin title
9. One SEO-optimized pin description
10. Suggested visual direction for the first pin
11. Notes on availability / competition

---

PINTEREST CONTENT RULES:

* Pinterest is SEARCH-FIRST, not clever-first
* Every product should generate MULTIPLE pins
* Follow: Hook → Value → CTA
* Titles must be keyword-rich and scroll-stopping
* Avoid vague or generic phrasing
* Lead with the OUTCOME, not the product name
* Favor low-effort language when true:
  * easy
  * fast
  * beginner-friendly
  * no mess
  * at-home
  * under $30 / under $50
* Prioritize searchable phrasing over generic hype
* If a product cannot support:
  * problem
  * aesthetic
  * list
  * transformation
  * how-to
  then it is a weak candidate

---

PIN PACKAGING RULES:

For each product, think in terms of outcomes like:

* save money
* save time
* look better
* feel more organized
* make home/backyard/travel setup easier

For titles and angles:

* outcome > product
* process > specs
* transformation > feature list
* search-intent phrasing > ad-sounding phrasing

Examples:

* Avoid:
  * “Gel Nail Kit”
  * “Outdoor Lights Review”
* Prefer:
  * “Salon Nails at Home in 20 Minutes”
  * “This made my backyard look expensive”

---

VISUAL STYLE (IMPORTANT):

All pins should follow:

* Clean Pinterest design
* Vertical 2:3 format
* Minimal clutter
* Soft white / beige backgrounds
* Product centered
* Soft shadows
* Bright natural lighting
* No logos or copyrighted characters
* Mobile-first readability
* Strong single focal point
* 3–6 word hook preferred on-image
* Clear visual hierarchy:
  * top = hook
  * center = product/result
  * bottom = benefit or simple support line

---

CONTENT FORMAT PRIORITIES:

For each product, favor these formats:

1. Problem / pain-point pin
2. Aesthetic / visual outcome pin
3. List pin
4. Transformation pin
5. How-to / process pin
6. Optional impulse-buy angle:
   * “Amazon made me buy it”
   * “under $30”
   * “worth every penny”

---

BONUS (BEAUTY / NAILS):

Include:

* Trending nail styles (spring/summer)
* Viral content ideas
* Transformation-based content angles

---

FINAL INSTRUCTION:

Prioritize products that:

1. Have highest revenue potential
2. Have strong Pinterest virality potential
3. Are easy to create repeatable content for
4. Match a female-skewed, mobile-first 18–34 audience
5. Can be framed around a fast, low-effort, visual outcome

Output at least 15 products ranked by:

* Revenue potential
* Trend strength
* Content scalability
```
