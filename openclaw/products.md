# PRODUCTS — Monetization Priority Map

## Core Rules

- Owned products always outrank affiliate products when both fit the same intent.
- Affiliate products are secondary by default.
- Do not choose an affiliate product if an owned product can satisfy the same click intent with a stronger margin.
- Do not force a product into content that does not fit its audience, use case, or visual logic.

## Selection Order

When choosing what to monetize:

1. owned product with strong intent match
2. owned product with moderate intent but strong visual fit
3. affiliate product with strong buyer intent
4. affiliate product with strong seasonal or visual fit

Reject selection if:
- the product is only loosely connected to the hook
- the visual cannot support the promise
- the monetization path feels forced
